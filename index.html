Two Minute Drill

You are the quarterback of the New England Patriots in the biggest game of the year, the Super Bowl. The opposing team has just scored to put you and the Patriots down three points with two minutes and fifteen seconds remaining in the game. The kick return has you and your team starting your drive at the 35 yard line.

It is up to you to take your team down the field and win the Super Bowl. It is 1st down and 10 with two minutes left in the game, your team has one timeout left. What do you do?

Your receiver catches the ball and gets the first down and more, a total of fifteen yards gained on the play. You now have another first down on the 50 yard line, with one minute and fifty seconds remaining. What is your next play call?

Your running back gets a gain of five and is able to get out of bounds, stopping the clock with one minute and 55 seconds left. It is now 2nd and 5 on your own 40 yard line. What play do you call next?

The pass is incomplete, which leaves you with a 2nd and 10 with one minute and fourty seconds remaining. Do you pass the ball again?

Your running back breaks free and gets the first down, but stays inbounds so the clock continues to run. Do you call another play or use your last timeout to stop the clock?

Your running back is able to get the first down and get out of bounds! You now have a first and 10 at the 40 yard line with one minute and 25 seconds remaining. Do you call another run here?

You run the ball again and come up just short of the first down with the clock winding down. Do you waste a down to spike the ball or use your last timeout?

You hit the receiver downfield for a gain of 15 but he is not able to get out of bounds so you decide to rush to the line and spike the ball. This stops the clock with a minute remaining, but you now have the ball on the opposing 35 yard line. Knowing that you have to score a touchdown to win the game, what is your next call?

You call the timeout with one minute and 25 seconds remaining. You get in the huddle and talk over the rest of the drive with your team. What play do you come out of the timeout with?

You get the offense up to the line quickly and spike the ball on second down. The clock is now stopped with a minute remaining. Do you try to catch the defense off guard with a play action pass?

You pass the ball and complete the pass for another gain of 15, but the clock continues to run. Do you spike the ball again or go for another quick pass?

You call the pass play, with your main look being a post route that will beat the safeties. You throw the ball to that route and its caught! Your receiver breaks away from the defender and scores the touchdown with minimal time remaining!

YOU HAVE WON THE SUPER BOWL!

The defense knew the run was coming and stops your running back right at the line of scrimmage. As he was falling to the ground another defender popped the football out of his arms, FUMBLE! The defense recovers the ball and takes posession, takes a knee, and lets the rest of the clock run out.

YOU HAVE LOST THE SUPER BOWL!
